Projects

Dominos Dashboard

I recently completed a data analytics project focused on analyzing Domino’s pizza sales to uncover insights into customer behavior, peak ordering times, bestselling categories, and overall revenue trends. By exploring over 21K orders and 818K in revenue, this dashboard provides a comprehensive view of how different pizza types, sizes, and timings influence business performance. This project helped me strengthen my analytical thinking while turning raw data into meaningful, decision-driving insights. To build this end-to-end dashboard, The final dashboard was developed in Power BI, leveraging DAX for calculated measures, interactive slicers, custom visuals, and drill-down capabilities. These tools together enabled me to transform a large dataset into an intuitive and actionable visual storytelling experience.
